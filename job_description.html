About Squad

Humans + A.I. = Future of Work

SquadVoice: When businesses sell expensive, high-ticket products or services to consumers (like real estate, insurance, mortgage), there’s barely any data-driven intelligence, personalization, and sales acceleration to drive better outcomes. Imagine every time you’ve gotten a bad quality phone call to sell you something like a credit card or a loan which could’ve been so much better. Now imagine that happening to millions of people across several industries. As a result, sales conversions are poor and it’s a massive missed revenue opportunity, not to mention terrible customer experience.It’s an exciting time to join us at Squad. Just 12 months into our launch in the US Real Estate market, we are growing incredibly fast (growing 30%+ MoM) and have the vision and plan to scale this to a billion-dollar opportunity, and beyond!

We’re looking for entrepreneurial people with the right mix of hunger, experience, and cultural fit to help us realize our vision of ‘re-imagining how work gets done’.

Responsibilities:

  • Ingesting data from various sources and manipulating it in the desired format
  • Identifying patterns and trends in data sets
  • Running statistical tests to determine the cause behind an observation
  • Automating and optimizing processes to reduce the time spent
  • Designing, running and analysing experiments to solidify insights

Role Requirements:

  • 0-1 years of experience.
  • Adequate knowledge of a scripting language like Python/R
  • Ability to fetch data using SQL
  • Familiar with Data Visualisation for exploration with tools/libraries like Seaborn, ggplot, Tableau etc.
  • Ability to articulate insights into presentable reports
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • An analytical mind and inclination for problem-solving
  • Detail oriented, analytical and inquisitive

Ability to work independently and with others

Logistics:

Location: Noida
